I love online education and the current landscape of EdTech, so much that I have decided to pursue a Masters in Science via an online program. To me, online education isn’t just innovating on the traditional classroom, it’s a universal and powerful democratization of ideas, intelligence, and experience. The lower cost of production, promoting consumer affordability, will undoubtedly help people from all backgrounds achieve previously unreachable academic success. I created this blog so I could track and rate the online classes, specializations, and degrees I’ve studied. Below are my reviews of the most popular EdTech platforms and courses I've completed, feel free to search by topic/tag/title with the search bar.
